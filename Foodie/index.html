Popular menu

Biryani is a spiced rice dish layered with marinated meat (like chicken, lamb, beef, or fish) or vegetables. It is known for its aromatic basmati rice, tender meat, and a blend of spices that create a rich, savory flavor. The dish has Persian origins but became popular in India, where it evolved with local flavors and ingredients, especially under the Mughal Empire.
